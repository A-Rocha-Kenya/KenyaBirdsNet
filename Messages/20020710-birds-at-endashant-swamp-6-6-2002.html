Subject: Birds at Endashant swamp 6/6/2002
Dear All

On Sunday 9/6/2002 there was a major influx of Lesser Moorhens (20+ seen and
many more heard) at Endashant swamp near the rifle range behind the Ngong
Hills, also at least one dwarf bittern seen. Conditions at the swamp
generally looked good for crakes.
