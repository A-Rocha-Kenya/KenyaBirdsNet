Dear all,

i recieved the following records from Nik Borrow,for a trip in 
November, via Brian Finch (and i hope that soon we will have the 
exact dates).

* Fox Kestrel in Shaba.
* Eurasian Wryneck in the garden at Naro Moru.
* A couple of Great Snipe at Kinangop.
* Lots of Williams's & Masked Larks in Shaba. 
* Lesser Jacana on that dam (what's it called?) between the Cape 
Eagle Owl & Nakuru.
* A pair of Red-necked Falcons at Sabaki River.
* Amur Falcon near Kichwa.
* vepres Black-lored babblers near Naro Moru (split them!). 
* Gambaga Flycatcher in the Kerio Valley. 
* Black-and-white Flycatchers actually on the nest in Meru Forest. 
* Southern Grosbeak Canary at Maktau Gate.

all the best for now, and happy birding to all
