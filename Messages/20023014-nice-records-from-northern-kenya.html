Dear Birders

Though this has taken quite some time it's worth to note these 
spotting during my trip to Samburu, Pokot and Turkana, that was later 
August and Sept.

1. A pair of Gambaga Flycatcher about 30km north of Archer's Post in 
samburu, that's on the slopes of Mt. Lolokwe.

2. Hemprich Hornbill in Sigor near Marich Pass.

3. 10 Caspian Terns on Lake Turkana's Elia springs.
