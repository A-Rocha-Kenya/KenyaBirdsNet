Dear all,

On Monday 25 November, Brian Finch reported a group of 20 (!) Stone Curlews, 100m outside the Irinito gate (off the pipeline road) in Amboseli NP. The birds had been discovered by Melogola (guide at East Afr. Orn. Safaris) and had been wintering in this area over the last 10 years! 

Good birding!
