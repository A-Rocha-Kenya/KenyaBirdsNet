Subject: Maccoa and White-backed Ducks
A lovely Wednesday Morning Birdwalk on 12 March 03 at Manguo Pond, Limuru

Among the birds seen in brilliant light were some 56 White-backed Ducks and
23+ Maccoa Ducks, plus a few Garganey, Northern Pintail and Northern
Shovelers in breeding plumage.

Good birding, Fleur
