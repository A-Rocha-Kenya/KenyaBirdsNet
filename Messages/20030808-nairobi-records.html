Greetings birders

Nature Kenya's Wednesday morning birdwalk had a good tally of birds on 6
August 2003 on the rocky hillside with seasonal springs and pools near the
Carnivore restaurant.  Among them were three Brown Parrots feeding on
Acrocarpus flowers and a Madagascar Squacco Heron feeding on tadpoles.

Good birding,
