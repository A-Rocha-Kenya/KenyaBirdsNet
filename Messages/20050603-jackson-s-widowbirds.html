Subject: Jackson's widowbirds
Nature Kenya's Wednesday morning birdwalk went to Nairobi National Park on
Madaraka Day, June first. The grass was high, green, topped with silver and
gold, and filled with Jackson's, Red-collared and White-winged Widowbirds.
