Hello Birders,
   
  Sorry that in my last Western Kenya trip report i didn't tell you where i saw those birds, sorry for that again. But this time, i send you where i saw those birds with the location.
   
  1.Kongelai Escarpment.
     Little Rock Thrush.
     Yellow-billed Shrikes.
     White-crested Turaco.
   
  2.Keringet Dam.
     Bronze-tailed Starling.
   
  3.Keiyo Trading Centre.
    Heuglin's Masked Weavers.
   
  4.Endebee, Kitale.
     Hartlaub's Marsh Widowbirds.
   
  5.Mt.Elgon N.P.
    Crested Guineafowls.
   
  6.Malava Forest Centre.
    Western Banded Snake-eagle.
   
  7.Kakamega Forest.
    Grey Parrots.
    Mackinnon's Fiscal.
    White-spotted Flufftails.
    Blue-headed Bee-eaters.
    Turner's Eremomelas.
    Red-bellied Paradise Flycatcher.
   
  8.Lake.Victoria Safari Village, Mbita.
    Northern Brown-throated Weavers.
   
  9.Ruma N.P.
    Blue Swallows.
   
  Happy Birding.
   
  Shailesh.
