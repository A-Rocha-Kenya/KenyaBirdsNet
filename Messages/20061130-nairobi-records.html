Nairobi records Greetings all

At the seasonal pool surrounded by large houses in Runda Estate, Nature Kenya’s Wednesday Morning Birdwalk had a quick glimpse of a Lesser Moorhen on 29 November, the first for many months.

That afternoon, a young Eastern Honeybird was begging from, and fed by, a pair of Abyssinian White-eyes outside my window in Nairobi.

Hope you all had a good World Bird Festival month, Fleur
