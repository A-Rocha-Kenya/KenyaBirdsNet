> This is to draw your attention to a new webbased
> database on Palearctic 
> migrants in Africa:
> www.macroecology.ku.dk/africamigrants. It is set up
> by 
> the Center for Macroecology of the University of
> Copenhagen in Denmark. The 
> site can be used to:
> 
> - download our publications on Palearctic migrants
> in Africa
> - download a list of Western Palearctic bird species
> migrating within Africa
> - download geo-referenced records on the
> distribution of Palearctic migrants 
> in Africa
> - enter new records for Palearctic migrants in
> Africa
> - find useful links to other websites dealing with
> bird migration
> 
> Much data that was brough together by observers over
> the last few years, 
> notably from observers in Kenya (by using the
> special migrant-forms, later 
> also from Birdfinder), went into this database. Much
> of the site is still 
> under construction and comments are welcomed by the
> makers. Please feel free 
> to spread this information further, for example on
> Kenyabirdsnet.
> 
> Initiatives like these seem to be popping up and I
> sometimes wonder how many 
> wheels are invented simultaneously in different
> places. Anyway, I suppose it 
> helps to be aware of and link up to promising
> projects as much as possible, 
> and this may be one of them.
