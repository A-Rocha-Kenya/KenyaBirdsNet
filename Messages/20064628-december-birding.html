Hello Birders,
 
Just did my last 2006 birding tour for 2 weeks. It is very green where ever you go and it rains like cats & dogs. Palaearctic and Afrotropical migrants species seen during this trip. Had a stop over at Kerio valley for lunch coming from Kitale going to Baringo.
 
Some of the species we saw at different areas.
 
1.Kakamega Forest.
  White-breasted Negrofinch - 1.
  Vieillot's Black Weaver - 4.
  Honeyguide Greenbul - 1.
This species were seen around Rondo.
 
2.Buffalo Springs N.R.
  Great-spotted Cuckoo - 2.
 
3.Mt.Elgon N.P.
  Doherty's Bush-shrike - 3.
  Grey Cuckoo-shrike - 3.
 
4.Lake Baringo.
  Masked Shrike - 1
Follow the road which passes Kampi ya samaki and go north along the lake shore. 
 
5.Kerio Valley.
  Black-headed Gonolek - 2.
  White-crested Helmet Shrike - 1.
 
6.Mountain Lodge.
  Eastern Bronze-naped Pigeon - 3.
  Kenrick's Starling - 3.
  Sharpe's Starling - 2.
  Common Whitethroat - 1.
 
Happy Holidays and see you in 2007.
 
Shailesh
