Thanks to Simon Thomsett we have an answer:

 

Mike.

Your bird was a 2 year old pale juv Ruppell's Griffon tagged on Game Ranching Athi River on 24th Aug 06.

This is an interesting record, showing a range we did not anticipate.

In addition this bird may be a hybrid between Ruppell's and the Eurasian Griffon. I do not think it is, but it look like those reported to be so.

The fading is very bad news for us. It seems that this is a problem with others in the Mara.

Many thanks to all.

Simon.
