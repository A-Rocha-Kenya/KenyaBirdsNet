Pallid Honeyguide in Karen Greetings

Nature Kenya’s Wednesday Morning Birdwalk spent a delightful morning at the Karen Golf and Country Club on July 25. Thanks to Karen Plumbe for arranging our visit.

Highlights were Pallid Honeyguide – a small pale honeyguide with a small head and thick pale bill, and a new bird for the Wednesday Morning Birdwalks – Black-collared Apalis and Violet-backed Starlings.

Wishing you good birding, Fleur
