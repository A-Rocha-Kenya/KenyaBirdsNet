Nairobi birds Greetings Birders

Nature Kenya’s Wednesday Morning Birdwalkers had an unexpected view of two Scaly Francolin at the Giraffe Centre Nature Trail at noon on this holiday birdwalk, 12.12.07. Also skies filled with swifts and swallows (African Palm, Mottled, White-rumped & Little Swift and Red-rumped & Lesser Striped Swallows, Rock & Common House Martin and Black Saw-wing).

Last week, 5 December, at Paradise Lost in Kiambu, Grey-Olive Greenbuls were foraging at the base of the waterfall.

The Sunday Birdwatch is on 16 December, Fleur
