Levaillant's Cuckoo in Naro Moru Greetings

Levaillant's Cuckoo seems to be getting around this season. One was seen on the forest edge at Bantu Lodge in Naro Moru on April first, by Catherine Ngarachu, Mike Davidson and myself, on the sidelines of a meeting.

The grounds of Bantu Lodge (Mountain Rock) had an interesting mix of highland and dryland species, with Red-fronted Parrot, Grey Cuckoo-shrike, Purple-throated Cuckoo-shrike, Montane Oriole and Thick-billed Seedeater together with a pair of Red-headed Weavers.

Wishing you good birding, Fleur
