Migrants at last Greetings birders

At last a few migrants have arrived in Nairobi.

On 28 September in Nairobi National Park, Green Sandpiper, probable Pallid Harrier female, Eurasian Bee-eaters and Northern Wheatear.

On 5 October in Nairobi National Park, Eurasian Hobby and a migrant from somewhere – a black morph Black-and-white Cuckoo.

Wishing you good birding, Fleur
