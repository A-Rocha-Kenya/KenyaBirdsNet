I had a golden-winged in Langata a few years ago and I believe it too was at this time of year – I will have to check my notes.  Up here on our place in Laikipia we have had a sudden influx in our area of Bare-eyed Thrush (three sightings in the last week).  Also Rufous Rollers seem to have arrived, after a time away, in the past three weeks.
On the mammal front we have seen two aardwolves in the past two days, a cheetah, four wild dog and three lion last week and we are currently under siege from a few hundred elephant determined to wreck all our pretty shade trees.
