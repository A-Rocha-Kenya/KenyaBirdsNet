Dear all:

On Friday 20th February, there was a Long-legged Buzzard soaring over the Sarit Centre in Westlands.

On February 22nd, (yesterday), while playing cricket at the Nairobi Club, I saw an Ayre's Eagle and a dark-morphed Booted Eagle (white-landing lights were unmistakable) flying in the grounds of the Nairobi Club, near Kenyatta National Hospital.

Happy raptoring to all :)
