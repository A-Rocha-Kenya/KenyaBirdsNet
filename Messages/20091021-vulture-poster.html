Colleagues

 

In a bid to raise awareness about the beauty of vultures and the plight they are facing, a poster has been produced by the BirdLife Africa Partnership Secretariat. This poster is currently being distributed through national BirdLife partner NGOs in Africa and other contacts. However, you can access and disseminate (print etc) a high resolution PDF copy of the poster at http://www.birdlife.org/regional/africa/pdfs/vulture_poster_highres.pdf

 

This poster has been produced with support from the Royal Society for the Protection of Birds (RSPB - the BirdLife International Partner in the UK) and the Rufford Maurice Laing Foundation. Feel free to use the poster as appropriate.

 

It may be possible to send copies to other institutions/individuals who may find it necessary to use several high quality copies. Contact me at paul.ndanganga@birdlife.or.ke
