Hi all
 
At long last a proper 3 year campaign to finally eradicate these birds is gathering pace. The basic plan is to have at least 1,000 traps in Dar supplemented by poisoning, roost shooting and a nest with chicks bounty.
 
I'll gladly forward full details to anyone wanting them.
 
Meanwhile please, if you live in Dar print out a few of these "fliers" and distribute them. We need those extra committed individuals to sign up as local neighbourhood coordinators.
 
To those who do not live in Dar please forward to your friends and contacts that do. Especially the non-birders who will not receive this msg. Everyone has a role here and it can be done. Over the years many individuals have killed thousands of birds but to no avail. This time saturation trapping will make the difference.
 
Those poor people that live in Dar can then begin to watch real birds in their gardens again.
 
Neil
