Subject: INTERESTING SIGHTINGS
On a visit last Sunday to the Lake edge on Hopcraft's Loldia farm Naivasha, a Ross's Turaco was positively identified by several of us.This had been reported earlier to me but thought to be inaccurate! 
Several Fish Eagle pairs were present including one perched on the back of a hippo, later joined by his mate and we witnessed an attack on a passing pelican who was forced to disgorge his just caught fish!
Also a recent visit to Nairobi Park over 5O Red Backed Shrikes were seen as well as more than lO Lesser Grey Shrikes, one Whinchat, two Whitethroats and several Spotted Flycatchers.
Good Birding !
Best wishes to all
