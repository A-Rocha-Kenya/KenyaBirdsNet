Dear All,
 
I am in Kenya most years in June, and have checked my records.
 
I have a record from Elementaita in June 2000 (although I can't find the exact date, it must have been early June). In the same year I had a record (again at Elementaita) on 7th July.
 
I have a recollection, but no confirmation written down, of seeing them at above the waterfall at T. Falls in May and/or June, and thinking they may be breeding in the area, but this is all supposition and memory, so not to be relied upon.
