Subject: Illadopses in Kakamega
Just on the 19 of March, I was on a trip to the Kakamega rainforest.  To my surprise I found 4 of the 5 different Illadopsis Species living in Kakamega in just one day.  I don't know if this is unusual, but I thought it was quite intriguing
Mountain Illadopsis - - Illadopsis pyrrhepta
Brown Illadopsis - - Illadopsis fulvescens
Pale-breasted Illadopsis - - Illadopsis rufipennis
Grey-chested Illadopsis - - Kakamega poliothorax
