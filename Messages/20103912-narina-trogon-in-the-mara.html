Hi All,

Despite several failed attempts to see this bird in the Mara this season,
Vicki and I finally clocked it at Serenget Campsite on 9th October (just 5
minutes drive from Naibor Camp).

What a jaw-droppingly beautiful bird it is!

A few more migrants around lately including a male Pallid Harrier on Oct 10th, several Hobby over, a sprinkling of
Willow Warbler in camp and many Northern Wheatear passing through this week.

Happy Birding!
