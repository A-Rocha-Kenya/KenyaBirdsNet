Some scattered records from Nairobi and Hunter's Lodge.

Hunter's Lodge, Kiboko near Makindu, 14 November 2010, observed by Fleur Ng’weno and Moez Ali:
  • 4 Grey Crowned Cranes – I understand the cranes were introduced there some years ago
  • 3 Retz’s Helmetshrikes – seen again after a long, long time

Nairobi Hill, December 5: Eastern Olivaceous Warbler at the bird bath

Nairobi National Park, Dec 5: Two White Storks perched on a dead tree
