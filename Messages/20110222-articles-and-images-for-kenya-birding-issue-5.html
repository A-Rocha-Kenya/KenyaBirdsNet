Subject: Articles and images for Kenya Birding Issue 5
Hi all,
It is about that time of year again when the office sends out a request for articles and images for Kenya Birding magazine.  As I have agreed to assist Nature Kenya as editor on this upcoming issue, I have the privilege to write to you on the same.

Let me reiterate that Kenya Birding consists of information on:

Birds, other species, birding, sites or places of interest, conservation news 

What do we need? 
  • Well, first it would be great to get more feedback on the magazine – was it of interest, useful, informative? Did its content help in anyway?
  • Also if you have any ideas, questions, things or articles you would like to see or recommend for the magazine, please let us know.
  • Editorial material can be in the form of comments, notes, news, articles and longer features; images should be high resolution .tif or .jpg files
  • Remember we are also nurturing a 'naturalist segment' – so if you have interesting observations or photos on insects, plants, amphibians... or know someone who does, get them to write in

If you consider contributing as described above, send me an enquiry and I'll get back to you with further guidelines.

Thank you for your amazing support.
Best wishes,
Catherine Ngarachu

PS: I look forward to receiving your contributions on or before Tuesday 5 April
