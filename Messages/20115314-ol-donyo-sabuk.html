We’ve made two recent trips up ol Donyo Sabuk, though not specifically for birding I noted the following species.

May 1, 2011

Brown snake eagle

Cinnamon-chested bee-eater

Cinnamon –breasted rock bunting

Black saw-wing

Common bulbul

Heard only: Hartlaub’s turaco and Yellow-whiskered greenbul

 

May 14, 2011

Brown-capped weaver

Immature Martial eagle

Eurasian honey buzzard

White-eyed slaty flycatcher

Hartlaub’s turaco

Heard only: Yellow-whiskered greenbul

 

Cheers, Darcy
