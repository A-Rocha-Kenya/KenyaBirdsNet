Hi Don et al.,

Just a brief response to your note on seedeaters. From 1964-1975 I lived in Uganda & Kenya (& was briefly in East Africa in 1978 & 2005) & I have only the following records of these species -

Serinus gularis Streaky-headed Seedeater

K  1973: River Suam at Nakijit: few 29 Jul.

U  1966: Moroto: common end Sep.

 

Serinus reichardi Reichard’s Seedeater

K  1971: Muhoroni: few 2 Aug.


Some of these may have been netted for ringing, but I haven't got access to my ringing data at present.


Not sure if this is at all helpful.


Clive
