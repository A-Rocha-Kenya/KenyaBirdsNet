Subject: Lesser Kestrels Migrating
On a brief excursion (7th April) to the Lumo Conservancy bordering Tsavo West we encountered lots of perched Lesser Kestrels (30+) with fewer Amur falcons (9) mixed in with them. We also observed a single Eurasian Hobby over the lodge at Lion's Bluff.

During the rainy first week of April  large numbers of European Rollers were on the move and Barn Swallows were everywhere in the Taita lowlands (Mwatate, Msau, Bura, Godoma, Lualenyi) . Small flocks of Eurasian Bee-eaters were also seen.

Also we observed a flock of 25 Open-billed Storks at Voi Safari Lodge on April 6th,  does this species undertake local or regional migrations? We've never seen them in such numbers at the Lodge before.

Pete Steward & Radhika Timbadia
