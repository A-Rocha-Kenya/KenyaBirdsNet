Grey Crowned Cranes have now been officially uplisted from VU to EN which means that 50% of the world population is expected to be extinct in the next 10 years!

 

Please be aware of these birds where ever you see them and try to send records with counts, localities and conservation threats to the list for follow up.

Grey Crowned Cranes have now been officially uplisted globally from Vulnerable to Endangered on the IUCN Red Data List.  See the new Grey Crowned Crane factsheet at BirdLife international: http://www.birdlife.org/datazone/speciesfactsheet.php?id=2785, and a list of the 2012 status changes: http://www.birdlife.org/action/science/species/global_species_programme/whats_new.html.
