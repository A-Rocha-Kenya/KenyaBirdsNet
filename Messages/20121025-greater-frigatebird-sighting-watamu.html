Subject: Greater Frigatebird sighting- Watamu
Hi Birders!
Today morning we had a sighting of an adult female Greater Frigatebird (Fregata minor) at the beach at A Rocha field study center (Mwamba) in Watamu, Malindi. We had great views of the bird as it circled literally overhead for more than five minutes before disappearing down the beach. I'll keep a lookout for it and hopefully next time I will have my camera!
