African Golden Oriole in Nairobi Greetings birders

An African Golden Oriole was the highlight of a Wednesday Morning Birdwalk in Karen, Nairobi, on August 8th 2012.

We also had great views of a Red-chested Cuckoo – silent, but followed by a thunderstorm this evening – and a Slender-billed Greenbul on the bird bath. Many thanks to Wendy Andrew.

Wishing you good birding, Fleur
