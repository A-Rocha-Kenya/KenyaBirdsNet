Crescent Island, 23.09.2012

Here is the list from our trip to Crescent Island, Sunday 23rd September. Very busy for birds, with some migrants including Northern Wheatear and Yellow Wagtail. It’s a great place to get close to mammals, we saw: Dik Dik, Waterbuck, Giraffe, Wildebeest, Grant’s Gazelle, Thompson’s Gazelle, Buffalo, Hippo, Impala, Plains Zebra.

We were a group of 5 people including Peter Steward, Radhika Timbadia, Wendy Ayres, Sukhy and Jaytinder Soin.

Pete and Radhika.
