Subject: Madagascar Pond Heron
Hi all
 
It's that time of the year again when MPHs migrate from their Madagascan breeding grounds to the African mainland.
 
this bird is in serious trouble, mainly due to pressure on its breeding grounds.
 
we can perhaps monitor the population by revisiting known sites on the mainland to see if they still support these birds.
 
Last Sunday we found at least 12 birds in and around a rice scheme on the lower reaches of the Ruvu Valley. This is an unprecidented number for us in a single location so PERHAPS they had only recently arrived. We will check out other rice schemes in the valley next weekend as well as revisting this one to see if the birds are still around.
 
This msg serves to alert everyone that these birds are now arriving so please find time to visit known sites and let us have any information including negative data and a comment on the quality of the habitat
 
many thanks
