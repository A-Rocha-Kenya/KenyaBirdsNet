Subject: Fish Eagles settle in Nairobi
Greetings birders

African Fish Eagles are sighted more often in Nairobi these days.

On Christmas Day, Nature Kenya's Wednesday Morning Birdwalk found two ­ an
adult and a sub-adult ­ at Rosslyn River Garden Café between Gigiri and
Runda.

Wishing you a birdfull new year, Fleur
