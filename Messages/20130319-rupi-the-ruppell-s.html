Dear all,

I thought I would give a quick update on the position of a Ruppell's Vulture we tagged last October. The bird was trapped and tagged in the Masai Mara and spent quite a lot of time near Lake Eyasi. It is doing fine and as at yesterday was about 24 km south west of Olduvai Gorge.
