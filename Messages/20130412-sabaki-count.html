Subject: Sabaki count
We did the monthly waterbird count at Sabaki today - and a very different count it was! The water level in the river is extremely high and even at fullest low tide there was only a narrow strip of mud flats exposed along the side - otherwise there were huge standing waves of basically mud flowing downstream - carrying more of Ukambani's shambas into the Indian Ocean...

The one thing to report bird-wise was 715 Madagascar Pratincoles - which is one of the higher counts we've had in recent years.

Very few migrants around this year - though we caught and ringed two Euro Golden Orioles together from the same net two days ago.
