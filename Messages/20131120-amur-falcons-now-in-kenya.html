Two of the Amur Falcons are now in Kenya - map attached.


You can keep track of the movements of the three tagged Amur Falcons by following the instructions below:

 

1.  Click on www.satellitetracking.eu

 

2.  Click on 'English' along the top menu bar to convert the language.

 

3.  Click on 'Birds' on the top menu bar.

 

4.  Search down Page 1 of the results and find - Naga, Pangti and Wokha.

 

5.  Click the little white boxes to the left of all three names.

 

6.  Go to the bottom of the page and click on Send Map.

 

You can then zoom in/out using the scale in the top left.   And reposition the map by clicking, holding down and dragging it across your screen.
