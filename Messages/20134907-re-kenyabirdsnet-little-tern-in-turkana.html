Hi All,
 
Exciting news! This has made me think again about my own experience on a trip to Lake Turkana in April 2001. I saw a flock of about 20 small terns with yellow bills with black tips sitting on the spit which juts into the lake just south of Koobiforra on the eastern shore. I was in a boat passing by (with a majority of non-birders!), and battling with a heavy swell, so we did not stop, but I automatically assumed that they were Saunders (following the literature of the day). Now it seems that they were more likely to have been Little. Can't be proved either way, but next time (I wish!) I'll insist on stopping the boat and having a good look. 
 
 Very best wishes to all,   Jeffrey
