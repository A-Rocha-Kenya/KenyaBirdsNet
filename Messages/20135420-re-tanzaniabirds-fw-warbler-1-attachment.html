Dear All,
Single shot birds are really difficult at times to identify,
especially half a bird. I would say that this is a first year Barred
Warbler. No white on throat, pale on bill, broken eye-ring, angled
forehead. The same angled forehead also is suggestive of a Hippo, but
no Hippo is that dark below.
Best for now
