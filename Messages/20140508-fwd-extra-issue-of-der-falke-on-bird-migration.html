Extra issue of DER FALKE on Bird Migration

Dear Colleagues and Friends

 

Please find attached the English translation of an extra issue on Bird Migration of the Magazine DER FALKE. You can also download this from www.vogelwarte-helgoland.de. We are distributing the pdf file (http://www.ifv-vogelwarte.de/downloads/Bird_migration_Falke_special_issue_2014.pdf) for free in order to make it available to a large number of people. In this issue, a number of experts present a range of papers on bird migration, stretching from a general overview of the current knowledge on bird migration, research methods, ringing, the German Red Data book of Migratory Birds, to wind energy, international conventions, and climate change. While some articles focus on Germany, we are sure most of the papers will be interesting for an international audience as well.

 

We hope you find our extra issue of DER FALKE on Bird Migration interesting. Please feel free to forward it to others who might be interested.

 

Best wishes,

 

Dr. Norbert Schäffer

Chief Editor DER FALKE

 

Prof. Dr. Franz Bairlein

Director Institute of Avian Research, “Vogelwarte Helgoland”
