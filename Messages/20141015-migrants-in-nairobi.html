Greetings birders

Nature Kenya's Wednesday Morning Birdwalks have recorded a number of
Palaearctic and inter-African migrants:

Flocks of Eurasian Golden Oriole and Eurasian Bee-eater on 8 October at
Rosslyn Lone Tree estate in Nairobi.

Today at Ngong Racecourse of the edge of Ngong Road forest:
Common (Steppe) Buzzard
Eurasian Bee-eater
Barn Swallow
Blackcap
Spotted Flycatcher
And Levaillant's Cuckoo
