I was doing a count of birds seen in or from my plot last Sunday.

With the afternoon assistance of B Finch we got to 67, which was one more than almost the same day a couple of years ago.

So far as migrants go, there were a lot of warblers, probably at least 20 willow, two whitethroats and several garden warblers whispering in the bushes.  Very encouraging.
