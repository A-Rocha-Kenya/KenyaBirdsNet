Dear all
 
The 2014 round of the Birdfair/RSPB Research Fund for Endangered Birds is now open, and will close on 30 September.

As always, these grants of up to US$2,000 are to support research work on birds listed by BirdLife/IUCN as Endangered, Critically Endangered or Data Deficient.
 
You can find further details and download an application form through the Birdgrants portal (http://www.birdgrants.org/), which also contains information on other grant-giving bodies.
 
Please forward this email to anyone you think might be interested in applying.
