Subject: Eurasian Wryneck and Amur Falcons at Lolldaiga Hills Ltd.
Hi,
 
On 26 Nov. 2014, Yvonne de Jong and Paul Benson observed a flock of ca. 150 Amur Falcons on Lolldaiga Hills Ltd...at ca. 6,200 ft asl (ca. 1,900 m asl).
 
On 28 Nov. 2014, Mike Roberts photographed a Eurasian Wryneck at Lolldaiga Hills Ltd...at ca. 6,400 ft asl (ca. 1,950 m).  This species is seldom recorded in Kenya.
