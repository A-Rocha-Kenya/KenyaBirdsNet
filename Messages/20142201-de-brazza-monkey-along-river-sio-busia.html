Subject: De Brazza Monkey along River Sio, Busia

I have a student who plans to carry out her thesis work on cryptic De Brazza monkey  along River Sio, around Mungatsi area and in Busia generally. The student basically wants to understand the current population status of this small sub-population and edge effects, other threats, (loss of riparian vegetation and other habitats, hunting, culture etc) and community attitudes towards its conservation.

I remember seeing this species a few years ago at Mungatsi. Has anyone of you seen it recently and anywhere within the  Busia grasslands area. Any documentation on the species from that area is also welcome. Please share with us.
