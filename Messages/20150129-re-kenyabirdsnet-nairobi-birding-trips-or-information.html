Some good birding spots in Nairobi that you can reach easily:

National Museums of Kenya grounds
Nairobi Arboretum
Uhuru Gardens National Monument
Karura Forest
Karen Blixen Museum and AFEW Giraffe Centre (both in suburb of Karen)

Wishing you good birding, Fleur
