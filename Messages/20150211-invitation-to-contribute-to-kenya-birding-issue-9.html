Greetings,
We are preparing for the 9th issue of Kenya Birding—an annual magazine about birds and birding; sites, places of interest, and conservation news.

We rely on this great birding community to make each issue special—please send in comments, articles, or sightings that you may wish to appear in the magazine.  These contributions can be:

• Short descriptions of sightings or other natural history observations
• Commentaries or news, in which you could draw attention to a conservation concern or a natural history event (between 50 and 350 words)
• Longer articles (between 350 and 700 words) or feature articles of 700-900 words

• Images should be high resolution .tif or .jpg files, and can be shared with me using the website: https://www.transferbigfiles.com—from this website send the files to catherine@brilliantlycrafted.com!!

(a) Let me know that you are thinking of contributing, and 
(b) Plan to submit by Wednesday 25 March 2015.

I'm very grateful for your support!

Catherine Ngarachu
Editor, Kenya Birding
