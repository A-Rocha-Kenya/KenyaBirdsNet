There have been a number of studies using niche modelling via Maxent to evaluate habitat changes and species ranges as a result of climate change. Attached is an example of a paper we published on the endangered Sokoke Scops Owl. 

Our African Raptor Data Bank with now over 100K plus records of raptors is preparing conservation assessment and habitat maps that can also be modelled to assess the impacts of climate change.
Having said that, Climate Change is the least of our worries for the moment as the African Landscape is set to change as a result of energy expansion which will have the most dire consequences for biodiversity.
