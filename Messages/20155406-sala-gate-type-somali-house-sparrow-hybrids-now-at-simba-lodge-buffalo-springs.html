group and staying at Simba Lodge in Buffalo Springs he found all
chestnut crowned Somali Sparrow types, that showed the greyish

light. It's possible that House/Somali Sparrows are increasing in
Somalia and the urge to wander in the genes of House Sparrow is
responsible for the spread putting our northern yellow Somali Sparrows
at risk,
