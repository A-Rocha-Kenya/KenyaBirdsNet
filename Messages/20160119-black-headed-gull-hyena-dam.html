Subject: Black-headed Gull - Hyena Dam

Hi all,


Had the luck today to be at Hyena Dam for a fleeting sight of a first winter Black-headed gull on Hyena Dam in NNP.  This is not listed in the Nairobi National Park Bird Checklist and Brian Finch informs me it is a new species for the park.


Happy birding this festive season.
