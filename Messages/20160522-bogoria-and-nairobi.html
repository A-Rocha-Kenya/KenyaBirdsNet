Greetings birders

A bird guide tells me that last week there were many thousands of flamingoes
on Lake Bogoria.

Locally, a Nature Kenya Sunday Birdwatch at Sukari Dam, by kind permission
of Brookside Dairies management, recorded Little Bittern and Greater
Painted-snipe. Also, both White-faced Whistling Duck and Yellow-billed Duck
with little ducklings.

Wishing you good birding, Fleur
