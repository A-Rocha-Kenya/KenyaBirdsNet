Dear Birders,

On 9th July we visited the Happy Valley Homes in Kipipiri, Nyandarua.

In the morning before visiting the Homes we did a short birding/Hike along the road to Mutubio Gate.

In the exotic plantation which  mainly consists of cypress trees, we saw two African Green Ibises.

They cooperated very well,for close to ten minutes, we had a very good view of the two, which to many was a Lifer!
