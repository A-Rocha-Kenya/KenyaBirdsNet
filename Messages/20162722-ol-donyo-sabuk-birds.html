Subject: Ol donyo Sabuk birds
Hallo all,

Stratton Hatfield and I went birding at Kilimambogo (Ol donyo Sabuk) this past week and got more interesting sightings: Northern Brownbul, African Hill Babbler, Golden-winged Sunbird, Eastern Bronze-naped Pigeon and two Bearded Scrub Robins (I am now convinced this is not a single lost bird, it is likely a breeding population).

Also a Wahlberg's Eagle at Urithi Estate, just about 5km from Blue Post, along the Gatanga road. This had been here the previous week too, where we had seen it with a group of Hinde's Babblers.

Thank you.
