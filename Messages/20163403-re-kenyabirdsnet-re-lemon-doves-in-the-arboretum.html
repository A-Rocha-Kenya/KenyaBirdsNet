Hi Sidney,

Uncommon perhaps but they are around if you know when and where to look. Also of interest, Grey Cuckooshrike has a very similar pattern of occurrence in Nairobi to Lemon Dove. Early October is possibly the best time to find them before they mostly depart for somewhere else in the middle of the month. Very rare at the Arboretum but better places to look are Ngong and Ololua Forests.

Nice find Sidney. Lemon Dove is primarily a dry season visitor to Nairobi. A handful of birds stay year round and may breed in the larger forest blocks (Karura, Ololua) but 90% of Nairobi records are Jan-Mar and Aug-Sep. The Arboretum is actually one of the more reliable spots in town. The map at this link gives an idea of citywide distribution:
