Subject: H Babblers breeding in Nairobi
Dear all,

A quick update on the Hinde's Babblers at the University of Nairobi's Upper Kabete field station. This week I have observed 5 individuals - 4 adults, 1 juvenile. This marks the first breeding record for Hinde's Babbler in Nairobi. Another interesting observation was a White-browed Coucal getting close to the babblers and being promptly driven away. I guess it is a brood parasite (or a potential one) for the babblers, as they are of a fairly similar size. See photos attached, though not high quality.
