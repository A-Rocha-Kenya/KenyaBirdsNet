Subject: Migrants in Nairobi
Greetings birders

Nature Kenya's Wednesday Morning Birdwalk, led by Peter Muriithi with Frank
Juma compiling the list, recorded several migratory birds from the north at
City Park in Nairobi today 11 October:

Common/Steppe Buzzard
Eurasian Bee-eater
Spotted Flycatcher
And also an Intra-African migrant, Black Cuckooshrike.

Wishing you wonderful migration birding, Fleur
