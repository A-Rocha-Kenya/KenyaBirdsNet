Subject: Migrants and more in Ngong Road Forest
Hallo all.

Today we had 3 sightings of Red-backed Shrikes in Ngong Road Forest. The southern migrants are surely in Nairobi. 

Several Willow Warblers seen. 
One Steppe Buzzard.
One Lesser Grey Shrike too.

Further, we witnessed a good battle between an Ayres's Hawk-Eagle and a pair of roosting Verreaux's Eagle-Owls.

The resident African Fish Eagles are still around; and last week we saw a subadult too. They should be nesting in the forest. 

Happy birding.
