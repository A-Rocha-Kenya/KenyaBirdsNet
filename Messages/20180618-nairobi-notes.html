Greetings birders

Nature Kenya's Wednesday Morning Birdwalk spotted a pair of Green-backed
Twinspots near the Kiambu Road picnic site of Karura Forest on a partly
sunny June 13.

On a foggy, foggy Sunday morning June 18 at Uhuru Gardens, the hardy
participants on Nature Kenya's Sunday Birdwatch recorded 63 species,
including Red-collared Widowbirds feeding fledglings, a small flock of
Chestnut Weavers and two White Storks who evidently didn't make the
migration. 

Wishing you good birding, Fleur
