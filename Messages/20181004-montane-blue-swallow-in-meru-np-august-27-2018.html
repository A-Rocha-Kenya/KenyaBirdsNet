Subject: Montane Blue Swallow in Meru NP (August 27, 2018)
Dear All,

I was flicking through Facebook yesterday and found a picture of a Montane Blue Swallow taken by Allan Welby in Meru National Park on August 27, 2018. I reached out to Allan and he said he took the photo somewhere between Elsa's Kopje and Rhino River Camp. I shared the sighting with Brian Finch and he thinks this record could be the first east of the rift.
