Hi Jamie,

I have 2 records of Magpie Starling (a few birds) and 1 record of Golden-breasted Starling (1 bird) over the past 6 years at Lolldaiga Hills Ranch...at ca. 1750 m asl near water in acacia bushland (A. mellifera et al. ). 

Similarly, only 1 record of Magpie Starling and, seasonally, a few records of Golden-breasted Starling on the Ewaso N'yiro River at Soita Nyiro Conservancy (ca. 1600 m asl.  Acacia bushland (A. mellifera et al.).

I do not recall seeing Fischer's Starling anywhere in Laikipia.

I would guess that these species are coming into the lower, drier, parts of Laikipia from the north (i.e., Isiolo and Samburu Districts).
