Subject: Kenya Birding 13: Call for contributions

Greetings all, I hope you are well and having a happy birdy new year.

We've the go ahead for another issue of Kenya Birding and I'm calling for your ideas.


The sections that have evolved in the magazine include:

Maildrop (brief birding observations)

Snapshot (a gallery of birds of a particular area)

Love Nature (brief natural history news, observations or experiences)

Urban birding (more detailed observations of birding in an urban environment)

Species (study or work around a particular species or family)

News (conservation related news)

Saving Bird Areas (descriptions of project work)

Fieldwork (stories from the field), and 

Where to Watch Birds (feature descriptions on places to go birding).


Please send me any ideas you may have or let me know asap (by FEB 22nd), of any contributions you would like to make for publication.
I will thereafter provide more specific details, in response on each idea or offer.


Many thanks, to all of you for your interest and support!! 

Best regards,
