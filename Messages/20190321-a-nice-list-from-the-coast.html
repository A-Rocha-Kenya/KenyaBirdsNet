Just a brief note here to draw your attention to a great list just sent in to eBird from the South Coast at Lunga Lunga by Mustafa Adamjee.

https://ebird.org/view/checklist/S54065211

Among stunning records of White-backed Night Heron and African Finfoot, there is also a photo of a Grey-olive Greenbul, at long last confirming presence of this species along the coast. It was reported north to Rabai District decades ago.
