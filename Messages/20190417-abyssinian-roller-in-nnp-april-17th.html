Subject: Abyssinian Roller in NNP April 17th
Dear All,

Just reported from Nagoloman Dam at 1600hrs on Apr 17th - a superb Abyssinian Roller, found and well photographed by Rupert Quinnell. I think there may be an old undated van Someren record but this is likely the first properly documented record for Nairobi. It could well be at the same spot tomorrow for those who want to chase it.
