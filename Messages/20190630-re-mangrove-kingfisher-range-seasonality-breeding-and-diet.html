Dear Brian. There are TWO POPULATIONS: Nominate birds are strictly Southern African, white our birds are ranivorus (Type locality Pangani).
Whether ranivorus is worthy of separate subspecific status is debatable however.

One thought being that nominate birds extends up to Dar-es-Salaam, Zanzibar and Pemba, with ranivorus from the Pangani River north to Somalia.

All depends on who you want to follow.
