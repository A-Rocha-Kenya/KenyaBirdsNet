As per Jennifer Oduori and James Kuria N'dungu (and others), Shikra have been breeding annually in the Makadara/Hamza area of Nairobi (between Embakasi and Kasarani) for some years now. It's a regular bird in the SE parts of the city with a few drifting westwards to the NNP and Karen-Langata.
