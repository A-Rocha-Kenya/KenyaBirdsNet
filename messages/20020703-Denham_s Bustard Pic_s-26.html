Anthony Wandera (Nature Kenya member) has sighted Denham's Bustard 
several times at Kuki Gallman's ranch in Laikipia. The bird has been 
sighted at least once a month. He has also kindly availed several 
good shots of the bird (using a Sony Digital Mavica FD 95 camera) to 
the office. As you probably already know, the bird is considered a 
threatened species in Kenya with reduced numbers (<300) and is 
largely confined to the Laikipia Plateau and high grasslands of 
northwest Maralal (Zimmerman et al). Happy birding!
