Greetings from the Wednesday Morning Birdwalk

Another nice record this morning: a Woolly-necked Stork at Rosslyn River
Garden Centre at Gigiri on the Limuru Road. I have seen Black Storks in the
Nairobi suburbs before, but not Woolly-necked (except for Nairobi National
Park). This one circled above the wetland, which is being renovated, and
then landed in an Acrocarpus tree above us.

Fleur
