Hallo Birders,
While counting Water birds in Nakuru we saw a crake
that we Identified us a spotted crake. In my team of
four I spotted this shy bird and quickly pointed it
out to my mates. The most stricking feature I noted
before sharing my pair of binoculars to one of us
was
- the yellow bill and the red base of the bill
especially on upper mandible, the spotted back,
greyish underparts and head and light under tail
coverts. Mwoki who we were counting with also saw
the
bird very well(we managed to take more notes too)
and
looking at the book and the description in the text
we
ended up councluding it to be a spotted crake!
This was below the WCK hostels shore line.
Nico.
