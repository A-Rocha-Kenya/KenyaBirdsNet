    9th Purple Grenadier, pair near Intrepids Lodge.
    10th Lesser Jacana, 1 Lily Pond Nr. T Falls
            White-backed Duck, several at above
             Purple Galinule, several as above
    13th Senegal Thicknee, L. Baringo
    14th Red-bellied Paradise Flycatcher, Kakamega

    17th Long-legged Buzzard, 2 Nr. Kisumu
            Brown-hooded Kingfisher, 1 Nr. Kisumu  A long way west of its normal range!
            Fischers' Lovebird, 1 Dunga Beach
    18th Lizard Buzzard, 1 Masai Mara NR   
    19th Woolly-necked Stork,  13 Musiara Swamp Masai Mara NR
    19th Long-toed Plover, 1 Musiara Swamp
