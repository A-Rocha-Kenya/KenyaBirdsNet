Hello all,

Bernd's recent note promted me to write a reply. 
Although I haven't been in 
Kenya since 1995 I birded the Loresho Ridge Swamp
regularly in the early 
90's.  I have also seen Madagascar Squacco Heron
there as well as other 
interesting birds such as Northern Anteater Chat,
African Stonechat and if 
my memory serves me correctly, Yellow-crowned
Canary.  During years when 
water levels were higher there, it was also an
excellent place to see Purple 
Swamphen.

James Bradley
