A few records from a recent Turkana truck trip with Gametrackers

Long legged buzzard      1 near El Molo Island Lake Turkana  30/12/2003
Masked lark                  4 Marsabit slopes and 1 in black lava near
Kalacha 27/12/2003
Brown babbler               4 Roberts campsite Baringo
Swainson's Sparrow       several Marasabit Town rubbish dump and Kalacha
campsite

Also

Booted eagle             1 light and 1 dark phase together Marsabit lodge
White backed duck     50+ Lake Paradise Marsabit 
Boran cisticola           several in song Marsabit lodge
House sparrow           A pair nesting at Marsabit petrol station
