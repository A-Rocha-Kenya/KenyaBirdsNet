Dear Itai,

I have been up to Keresoi Dam this morning and am
glad to report that the whole area is in pretty good
shape. There is some extra farming going on in one
area to the south of the dam and across the fence
but there is still a large area which remains
relatively untouched. 
The even better news is that there were sevearl
sightings of Aberdare Cisticola - some from very
close range. Also present were Sharpe's Longclaw -
in very respectable numbers.

Other birds of note

Wing Snapping Cisticola
Northern Pintail
African Snipe
Black Winged Plover
Red Capped Lark
