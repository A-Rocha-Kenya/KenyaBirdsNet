Hello Birders
I'm just reporting a few sightings some well old.

Today 21st Oct 04 had an Spotted Eagle Owl sub spp
africanus 2m from the Nature Kenya's kitchen window. 

Two African Hill Babbler at Arboretum yesterday 20th
Oct 04. I was with a birder by the name Tim.

Then On tuesday 12th Oct had 3 Olive Pigeons at Lake
Nakuru National Park on the Yellow Fever Acacia
woodland near the main gate

Then on 23rd Sept 04 had about 400 Jackson's
Widowbirds, 3 Aberadare Cisticola, 4 Sharpe's Longclaw
(2 kenya endemics and one east african endemic) in a
single farm in Kerusoi Molo grassland. The widowbirds
were in breeding plumage and displaying. 
Good luck
