Greetings

A delightful outing by the Nature Kenya Sunday Birdwatch to Karura Forest on 18 December recorded Wahlberg's Honeybird and two beautiful Grey Cuckoo-Shrikes, among others. Mammals sighted were Sykes Monkey, Unstriped Ground Squirrel, Ochre Bush Squirrel and Bushbuck!

An impromptu visit to Aberdares National Park December 10-11 recorded flocks of Slender-billed Starlings near Chania and Karuru Falls – and a black serval on the moorlands.

Wishing you all happy holidays and good birding, Fleur
