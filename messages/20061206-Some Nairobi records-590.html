Some Nairobi records Greetings to all

Nature Kenya’s Wednesday Morning Birdwalk visited the University of Nairobi demonstration plots at the end of Loresho Road, Lower Kabete, on a brilliant sunny morning, 6 December 06. A few highlights:

Little Bittern
Eurasian Bee-eaters
Red-throated Wryneck
Whinchat
And a flock of about a hundred Jackson’s Widowbirds of all ages in all stages of plumage, together with a few Red-collared and White-winged Widowbirds

Wishing you good birding, Fleur
