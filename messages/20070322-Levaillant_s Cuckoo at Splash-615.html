Levaillant's Cuckoo at Splash Greetings

Nature Kenya members on the Wednesday Morning Birdwalk of 21 March spent a delightful morning at Splash and its constructed wetlands. The whole group had close-up views of a handsome Levaillant’s Cuckoo and three or four Diederik Cuckoos.

At Karura Forest on the Sunday Birdwatch, highlights were African Crowned Eagle calling and displaying, Eurasian Hobby and Eurasian Bee-eaters.

Wishing you good birding, Fleur
