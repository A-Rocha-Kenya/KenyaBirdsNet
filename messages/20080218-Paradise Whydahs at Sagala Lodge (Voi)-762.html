Dear All
On a recent Succulenta trip to Sagala Lodge (to look for succulents!) we were amazed to discover in several acacia trees in the grounds of the lodge about 4O plus Paradise Whydahs in their breeding plumage as well as several Straw-Tailed and Steel-Blue Whydahs; an incredible sight.
Sagala Lodge was a very good birding stop ..maybe due to the unseasonal rain in the area
Best wishes and good birding
Karen Plumbe
