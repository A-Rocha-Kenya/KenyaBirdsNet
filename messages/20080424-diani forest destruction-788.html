Read details from http://colobus. wildlifedirect. org/ about how parts of forest in Diani, coastal Kenya are being destroyed by a hotel; with obvious impacts on birds  - home to some globally-threatened ones as well. During a 1-hr visit to the forest patch in question in July 2003, I saw the following species. We were  searching for the Spotted Ground Thrush (which we found in an adjacent property named 'Banana Farm')
