Nairobi National Park Greetings

A short morning drive in Nairobi National Park under grey skies found some interesting records:

A pair of Ostriches with more than 15 very young chicks, about the size of francolins.
A pair of Martial Eagles sitting on their nest in the riverine trees near Lone Tree.
A female African Finfoot, in the small stream near number 17.

Wishing you good birding, Fleur
