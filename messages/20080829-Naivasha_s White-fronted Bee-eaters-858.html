Don Turner has just informed me that following a rise in temperature, followed by a week of rain, White-fronted Bee-eaters are now returning to their favoured sites around Lake Naivasha.  Don say that the local bee population is also showing a marked improvement.  Hopefully things will return to normal for this species before too long.  Fascinating to speculate where they went though.
