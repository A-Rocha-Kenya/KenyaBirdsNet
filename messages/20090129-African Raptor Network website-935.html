The African Raptor Network website is finally official!!

You may view the site at www.africanraptors.org

I would like to thank Rob Simmons, Neil Deacon, Simon Thomsett, Laila Baha-el-Din, Robbie Whytock, Bethan Morgan and Christiane Trierweiler for their excellent contributions.
