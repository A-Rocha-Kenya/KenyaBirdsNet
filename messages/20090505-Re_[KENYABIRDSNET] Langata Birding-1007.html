The Institute of Primate Research, under the National Museums of Kenya, is a premier biomedical research institution. For more information, visit www.museums.or.ke  

Permission to visit the Nature Trail at IPR is obtained through the Museum’s Education Dept.

Better yet, join us on the Wednesday morning birdwalks! We meet at the Nairobi National Museum’s staff car park off Kipande Road at 8:45 am every Wednesday, and visit a variety of sites in all parts of Nairobi, including the Oloolua Forest.

For more information, join Nature Kenya, www.naturekenya.org
