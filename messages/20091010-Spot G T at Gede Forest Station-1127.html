...just had a report today that the forest guides saw a Spotted Ground Thrush under the trees around the offices at the forest station. First time I've heard of one there - though they used to apparently hop around the Gede Ruins offices back in the days when there were plenty of them (c.15-18 years ago now).

Still plenty of Euro Golden Orioles around and caught an immature male Fiery-necked Nightjar on the drive into our house this week which is also the first I've seen along the sea front.
