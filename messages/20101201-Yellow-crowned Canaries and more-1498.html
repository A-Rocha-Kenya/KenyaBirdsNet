Yellow-crowned Canaries and more Greetings

December 1st saw another fabulous Wednesday Morning Birdwalk to the University of Nairobi's Field Station at Loresho. Despite the large size of our group, we saw almost all of the area’s specialties, including:

Red-chested Cuckoo
Red-throated Wryneck
Jackson’s Widowbird
Crimson-rumped Waxbill
And dozens of Yellow-crowned Canaries, more than I have ever seen in Nairobi

Wishing you good birding, Fleur
