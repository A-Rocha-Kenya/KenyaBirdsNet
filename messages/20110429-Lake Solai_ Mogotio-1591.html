Further to emails earlier in the year regarding Lake Solai near Mogotio, I wrote to Ian Marshall who used to live and work on a sisal farm near there to see if he knew of the site and the birds on it. He responded with the following which may be of interest to those who have visited there (does anyone have a separate record of the Woodchat and Masked Shrike records for this site?):

Colin.

So herewith additions to the Lake Solai Area summary of birds from our
1960-80 lists:
Kittlitz's plover
D'Arnauds Barbet
Long-toed Plover
Black-winged Plover
Senegal plover
Temminck's Courser
Heuglin's Courser
Magpie Starling
Woodchat Shrike
Red-tailed Chat
Black-chested Snake Eagle
Brown Snake Eagle
Swallow-tailed Kite
Masked Shrike
Sandy-backed Pipit
Saddlebill Storks
seasonal Rock Thrush - females about 10 days before the males
Darters disappeard round the 70s after being a common visitor
