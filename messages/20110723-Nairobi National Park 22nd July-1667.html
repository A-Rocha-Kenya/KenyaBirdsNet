Dear all,
 
Johan and I went yesterday to Nairobi National Park. The weather was cloudly and cold.
 
Highlights :
 
3 Common Buttonquail on the main road to Hyena dam
 
The Eurasian Roller is still in the park near Karen dam
 
Athi dam provides 1 Common Sandpiper and 5 Wood Sandpiper
 
Hyena dam provides Common and Lesser Moorhen and the Madagascar Pond Heron
 
We finally got a good view of three wonderful striped Hyena near Karen dam.
 
A single Eurasian Hoopoe feeding next to an African Hoopoe near Athi dam.
 
The White-backed Night-Heron apparently left his place at Hippo pool.
 
Wishing you good birding,
 
Patrick
