Greetings birders

An Eastern Olivaceous Warbler has been singing its scratchy song in my
Nairobi garden since 18 January.

At Uhuru Gardens National Monument on Nature Kenya's Wednesday Morning
Birdwalk March 21, there were Barn Swallows, Eastern Olivaceous Warblers,
Willow Warblers, Spotted Flycatchers, Nightingale and Common Rock Thrush. On
the 23rd there was a Common Whitethroat and a local wanderer, a stunning
Beautiful Sunbird.

Wishing you good birding, Fleur
