As per group activity,every first saturday of the Month, Arabuko Sokoke Forest Guides Association (ASFGA) conduct monthly birdwalk. It is open to all wishers to join the group. You need to pay for your park entry at the Ticket office which is also the meeting point.It starts at 6.30a.m.
For this month of May, participants were, Willy Kombe,Ali Leslie,John Ngumbao, Susan Wanjiru and I.
