On Sunday 26th had a walk in Karura Forest,In the Lily Lake had a good view of the White backed Ducks(3 all adults)Common Moorhen, Little Grebe,Grey Heron,Hartlaub's Turaco. 

Black duck near the Waterfalls,Brown chested Alethe,African Emerald Cuckoo calling in the huge and tall Manikala discolor trees ,Grey cuckoo shrike,Cabanis's and Yellow Whiskered Greenbuls


Plus two Raptors namely: African Goshawk and Ayres's Hawk Eagle 

Enjoy Birding.
