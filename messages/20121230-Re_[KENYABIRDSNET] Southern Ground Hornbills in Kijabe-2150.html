Very exciting. We used to see Southern Ground Hornbill regularly in the Rift, around Naivasha, Gilgil, Nakuru, but not recently. It’s great that they are in Kijabe again!

You know that they are now considered threatened? They are listed as Vulnerable in the IUCN Red List.
