Last week observation at Kurawa Tana Delta at least five Madagascar bee - eaters on the high voltage electric wire, sitting near with carmine bee eaters.
Flamingos good number sighted at the salt pans along marereni gogoni area.
Eurasian rollers along the electric wire from Tarasa to Mijila lamu junction at list 8 counted, 4 lilac breasted, 7 striped kingfisher and 3 Grey headed kingfisher.
