I have attached photos of  what I believe to be a Little Tern photographed at Lake Turkana. There was a flock of about 80 of these birds. As can be seen from the photo, the white of the forehead extends to the rear of the eye giving a V-like profile unlike the Saunders's tern.

According to the guidebook I'm using (Zimmerman), there only 4 records so far from the coast and Naivasha. Does anyone have information as to whether there have been further sightings and from where they have been made?
