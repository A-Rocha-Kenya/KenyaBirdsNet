Hello everyone,

I've been asked about the next issue of the annual magazine Kenya Birding, and I'm now able to say that this issue has now been confirmed by Nature Kenya. 

Kenya Birding is about birds and birding; sites or places of interest and conservation news.  Types of articles needed are:

• Short personal notes or 'letters' to the editor
• Commentaries or news, in which you could draw attention to a conservation concern or a natural history situation (between 50 and 350 words)
• Longer articles (between 350 and 700 words) or feature articles of 700-900 words
• Images should be high resolution .tif or .jpg files and can be uploaded to this dropbox folder: 
https://www.dropbox.com/sh/euw5gbj01clml33/fgDgaSEJgE

We are later than usual in calling for contributions, but I hope we can still recover some time. It would greatly help if you could (a) let me know that you are thinking of contributing an article (at catherine@brilliantlycrafted.com) and (b) submit it by Friday 26 April.

I look forward to hearing from you and as always, thank you for your amazing support.
