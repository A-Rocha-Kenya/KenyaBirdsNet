Greetings birders

Nature Kenya's Wednesday Morning birdwalk recorded several Palaearctic
migrants today April 3 on the grounds of the Museum and the adjacent Nairobi
Rivers "Michuki" Park:

Common (Steppe) Buzzard
Willow Warbler
Garden Warbler
Common Whitethroat
Spotted Flycatcher

Kevin Gichuki also told us that the pair of African Black Duck who live on
the Nairobi River have hatched seven small ducklings.

Wishing you good birding, Fleur
