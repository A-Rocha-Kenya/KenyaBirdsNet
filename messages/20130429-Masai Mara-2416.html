One Beautiful Lesser Grey Shrike in full colour near Governors’ Camp, Masai Mara on 26th April. There are also Madagascar Squacco’s in the Musiara Marsh and one at the Murram pools near the Musiara airstrip on the 26th. Lots of nesting going on. Rattling Cisticola nest 3 eggs, Tawny-flanked Prinia nest building, Lesser Striped Swallows, many nest building, Spectacled and Holubs Golden Weavers nest building. All at Governors’ camp 24th -28th April.  40 plus Spur-winged Plovers on flooded road between Governors’ Camp and Musiara Airstrip.
