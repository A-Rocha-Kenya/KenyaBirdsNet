Since a couple of monhts I am a validator for Observado.org. This is a good tool to fill in your sightings for birds or other fauna or flora. It can be used for ranging species and interesting sightings. It could also promote bird watching on local en international level since more and more people are using it. The goal of observado.org is to make available sightings of observers all around the world and help with knowledge on biodiversity. Not only citizen scientist, but also professional researchers share their data on this website.
