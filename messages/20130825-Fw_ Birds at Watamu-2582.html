Dear All;
 
Apart from the ubiquitous Indian House Crows which are taking over, I recorded Eastern Nicator, Mouse -coloured Sunbird in nest, Silvery cheeked & Trumpeter Hornbills , Green Coucal & Red-capped Robin Chats at No 34. The Roseate Trens are nesting this year on the Turtle rock at Turtle Bay & on the southern-most rock at Whale Rock at the mouth of Mida Creek.
We also have a male Paradise Flycatcher of the white variety: a veritable prince amongst birds...
 
Best, Will Knocker
