Flock of Yellow Vented Bulbuls
Thu Jun 26, 2014 5:11 am (PDT) . Posted by:
"Kieran Avery" ruabora
Hi Everyone

Yesterday in the garden in Karen at around 8.00am I counted a flock of 42
yellow vented bulbuls (over a two minute period) all flying in the same
direction. They were vocalising excessively as though they were in a frenzy.
Unfortunately they didn't land anywhere in sight.

I have never seen a flock this big together at one time before so was
wondering if anyone else had ever witnessed this and if there may be an
explanation?
