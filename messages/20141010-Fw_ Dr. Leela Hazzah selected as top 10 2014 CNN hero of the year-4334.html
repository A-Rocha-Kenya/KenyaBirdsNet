Dear all,
I am forwarding an email from Nature Kenya indicating that Dr. Leela Hazzah of Lions Guardians has been selected as a top 10 CNN Hero of the Year and she is therefore nominated for the CNN Hero of Year Award.  She needs our votes each and every day before Nov 16. 

Leela and her team have done amazing work conserving lions in East Africa.  Of course there is a very strong link between the conservation of lions and birds.  Anytime a lion is poisoned, tens if not hundreds of birds of prey are poisoned too, including eagles, bateleurs and vultures. 

Leela is the only nominee tackling an environmental issue.  Let's help bring conservation to the fore and vote for Leela.

Best, Darcy
