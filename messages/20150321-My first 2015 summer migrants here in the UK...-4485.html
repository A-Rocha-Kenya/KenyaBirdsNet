Just out of interest - I saw my first summer migrants to the UK yesterday (bearing in mind I am living in London so less likely to see most migrants here). Two chiff chaffs battling it out with their voices in a tiny patch of woodland near Twickenham stadium. Their call is a good sign that spring is well on its way! I was surprised to see them in such an isolated pocket of habitat.


Looking forward to a week of birding in Kenya over the Easter period.
