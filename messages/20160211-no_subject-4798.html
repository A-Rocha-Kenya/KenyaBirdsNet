On a recent trip to the coast passing through Tsavo East to Vipingo and Watamu we had a very unproductive visit to Arabuko Sokoke forest.
However, on the way back we spent one night at Ngutuni Lodge near Voi where the birding was fantastic. Flocks of Wattled and Fishers starlings and Red Billed Buffalo Weavers but highlights of our brief visit was breeding Secretary Bird on nest, Northern and  Isabelline Wheatears, many Eurasian Bee Eaters and the highlight a Black Coucal showing off his orange rufous wings.

Good birding to all
Karen and Patrick Plumbe
