Excellent photo of the race schistacea which is common throughout the Persian Gulf from southern Sinai south to the Somalia and north Kenya coasts. Meanwhile there is also an inland population that occurs alongside typical Little Egrets (garzetta) along the Ethiopian Rift Valley lakes south to Turkana, Nakuru and Naivasha.  Dark birds are quite common in Ethiopia, also a few at Turkana, but this is the first I have heard of from Naivasha, though white phase birds have occurred from time to time.  Well done Sammy, and thanks for the great photo.
