Last Monday, Fleur Ng'weno, Ben Allen, Jennifer Oduori and myself spent a
birding day in Nairobi National Park.

The day proved to be very interesting with 157 species recorded.

More interesting were records of: over 20 Great White Pelicans roosting on
Eucalyptus trees at the main gate, a rare black-billed + black-legged Cattle
Egret and Yellow-billed Oxpeckers on a Warthog (while they don't often feed
on small mammals).
