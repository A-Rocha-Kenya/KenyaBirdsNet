Greetings birders

The Purple-crested Turaco has moved from the Arboretum to Ngong Road Forest
at the Racecourse! It had not been seen at the Arborteum for some months.
Today 13 July, Nature Kenya's Wednesday Morning Birdwalk had a good view of
it feeding on a Euclea tree.

We also saw a young Crowned Eagle near its nest, and 65 other bird species,
on a cold, grey, overcast morning.

Wishing you good birding, Fleur
