Nice find Sidney. Lemon Dove is primarily a dry season visitor to Nairobi. A handful of birds stay year round and may breed in the larger forest blocks (Karura, Ololua) but 90% of Nairobi records are Jan-Mar and Aug-Sep. The Arboretum is actually one of the more reliable spots in town. The map at this link gives an idea of citywide distribution:
