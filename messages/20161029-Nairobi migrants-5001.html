Greetings birders

Nairobi is a palette of colours with flowering trees right now, and
migratory birds are arriving:

At the National Museums of Kenya grounds Oct 18, Eurasian Wryneck ringed by
the Nairobi Ringing Group.

In City Park on Oct 26: Common (Steppe) Buzzard, Eurasian Bee-eaters,
Spotted Flycatchers, Isabelline (Red-tailed) Shrike.

In the Nairobi Arboretum on Oct 29: Grey Wagtail along the river.

And in my upper hill garden today, an immature Amur Falcon with red feet!

Wishing you good birding, Fleur
