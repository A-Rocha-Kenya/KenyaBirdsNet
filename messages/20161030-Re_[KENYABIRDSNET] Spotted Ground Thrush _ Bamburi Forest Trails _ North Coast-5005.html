When I set out on Oct27th to see what Bamburi Forest Trails was
harbouring

about 30Min. before it flew off into dense undergrowth. I went back
yesterday to try my luck again but it was not to be.
GPS co-ordinates are available in case anyone is interested. I also
mapped
the bird & submitted the card (pentad 0355_3940) to KBM.
