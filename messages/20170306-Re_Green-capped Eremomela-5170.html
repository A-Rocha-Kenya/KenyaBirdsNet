Dear Nigel,  This is indeed a fantastic record of the little known endemic race kikuyuensis, which Van Someren collected from Nairobi (believed the western suburbs) in November 1916, but did not describe it until 1931. It is very localized in the Thika, Embu and Meru districts, but has not been recorded from Nairobi for a very very long time, in fact I doubt there are any dated records.    Well worth writing up for Scopus.
