Yesterday morning, I was able to do some birding along the Mbagathi River/Ololua Forest edge below Mbagathi Ridge.  Quite unexpectedly, I got a very good view of a Green-capped Eremomela feeding in a Newtonia tree.  On getting back home, I discovered that it is a rare sighting for Nairobi and therefore worth mentioning on the Birdnet.
