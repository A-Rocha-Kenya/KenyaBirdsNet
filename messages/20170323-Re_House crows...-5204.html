The huge increases that we are seeing in House Crows is directly related to the increase in uncollected garbage that litter so many of our coastal towns.  In addition to the control efforts already underway, surely an equal amount of effort should go into getting the local councils to re-install a proper refuge collection unit, that would go a long way in reducing crow numbers. Just look back to the times when these councils did have efficient garbage disposal teams (not that long ago) and compare crow number then to what they are today.
