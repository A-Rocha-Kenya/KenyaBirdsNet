Dear Birders since last year 2016 we had these two species nesting at one of our man made lake island at Windsor Golf Hotel and Country Club. just when i joined the Place on June i had been recording great numbers of the Ibis which are over 200 Sacred Ibis and more than 20 Black- Crown Night- Heron. Sacred Ibis occupied the island till early November when the had finished raising the young ones.
From 8th May 2017 Sacred Ibis first seen around 200 of them. i will keep an eye to them till they number comes down to 2 or 4 as usual. Many questions are being asked by people where do they come from and if they are Migrants?
