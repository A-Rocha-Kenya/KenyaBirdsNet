A good paper to read is that by Peter Mundy at the Nairobi PAOC in 1988, entitled “The Problem of birds as pests in Zimbabwe”

Copy of the Proceedings is in the Museum Library as well as in the Ornithology Dept.

The paper is shown on pages 271-275.

Re queleas  both Queletox and Lebaycid (both containing fenthion) are legal avicides.

This might be a good starting point for those attending any proposed meeting.
