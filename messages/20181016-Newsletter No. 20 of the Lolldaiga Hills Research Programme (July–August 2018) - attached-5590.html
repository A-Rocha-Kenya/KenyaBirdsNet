All previous newsletters are available at:  www.lolldaiga.com

 

This website also holds the latest species lists for Lolldaiga Hills Ranch, including the Bird List (407 species) and Mammal List (99 species).

 

An estimated >300 elephants and >3,000 plains zebra are on Lolldaiga Hills Ranch (ca. 200 km²) during some times of the year.

 

Lolldaiga Hills Ranch holds the largest number of larger mammals (>3 kg body weight) of any site of its size in the world.

 

Lolldaiga Hills Ranch is open to visitors, including day-visitors and campers (www.lolldaiga.com).
