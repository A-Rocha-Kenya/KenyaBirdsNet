Greetings birders

Today 14 November Nature Kenya’s Wednesday Morning Birdwalk went to the Arboretum. A little disappointing for new participants as active birds stayed hidden in the foliage, but gorgeous trees in bloom and some good records:

Crowned Eagle - a pair calling and displaying overhead; Common Sandpiper along the polluted river; Levaillant’s Cuckoo; and an Amethyst Sunbird catching and eating a spider.

Wishing you good birding, Fleur
