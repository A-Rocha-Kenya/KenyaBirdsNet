Greetings birders

Nature Kenya’s Wednesday Morning Birdwalk stayed on the grounds of the National Museums of Kenya this morning March 6, and saw migratory birds preparing to fly north. They included:

Green Sandpiper and Wood Sandpiper
Willow Warbler – dozens singing and feeding
Eastern Olivaceous Warbler
Common Nightingale
And our goal – the vagrant Pied Flycatcher

Wishing you good birding, Fleur
