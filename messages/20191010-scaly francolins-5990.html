Hi all,

I had a nice morning birding in the rain in Thika. I was finally able to
confirm breeding of Scaly Francolins.  The two adults had two young ones in
tow.  I hear and see francolins much more often and despite all the habitat
destruction, both on Del Monte and elsewhere in Thika this population
appears to be doing okay.
